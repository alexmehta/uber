Benefits

Safe Journey

A journey with us always a safe one. We thoroughly screen and vet all our drivers through a multiple rounds of scrutiny. First, all drivers must undergo a standard background check. Next, drivers must pass a 1-hour written exam with a score of 80% of higher. And last, they must pass an in person driving exam.
